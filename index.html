The inspiration behind the Aether brand comes from the intangible elements in the atmosphere, symbolizing purity, transparency, and agility. Aether's design style is based on minimalism and modernity, seeking a clean, lightweight, and elegant feel. Each piece of Aether jewelry emphasizes material selection and lightweight design, showcasing the natural freshness and agility of the jewelry through simple shapes. The brand's signature characteristics are simple shapes and streamlined designs, making each piece of jewelry akin to the air—natural and elegant—and giving wearers a sense of lightness and ethereality.

Aether brand's products cover a variety of materials and design styles to showcase the brand's concept of purity and agility. Here are some popular series of Aether brand:

  • Aura Series
  • Lumina Series
  • Serene Series
  • Serenity Series

And among them, the Serenity series is Aether's abstract art collection, with a passionate and creative designer behind it named Elena. Elena has a keen eye for capturing the subtle details of the natural world and expressing them through abstract design language. She observes the ripples formed by water droplets on a lake's surface and transforms them into flowing lines and minimalist shapes, creating the unique designs found in the Serenity series. The Serenity series represents Elena's pursuit of inner peace and contemplation. Each design is her interpretation of balance and harmony in nature, and every detail in the jewelry is a tribute to tranquility and beauty.

On the 28th of this month, we are hosting a special event in collaboration with designer Elena to celebrate the launch of the Serenity series and Elena's creative achievements for the Aether brand. Throughout the entire day, guests will have the opportunity to admire the Serenity collection up close and experience the serenity and depth conveyed by each design. They will be able to personally try on the jewelry, immersing themselves in the graceful and elegant ambiance that aligns with Aether's brand philosophy. In addition to the art exhibition and interaction with the designer, the event will also include musical performances and culinary delights for guests to enjoy.
