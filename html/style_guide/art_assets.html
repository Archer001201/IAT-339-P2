Cinzel for Logo

Aether has chosen the Cinzel font for their logo. Cinzel is a typeface known for its elegance and classical appeal. With its bold and distinctive letterforms, Cinzel adds a touch of sophistication and timeless beauty to Aether's logo. In addition, the use of the Cinzel font in Aether's logo also adds a hint of mythical beauty. The elegant and intricate details of the Cinzel font evoke a sense of enchantment and wonder, lending a touch of ethereal and mythical aesthetics to Aether's brand identity. This subtle element further enhances the overall appeal and allure of the logo, capturing the imagination and creating a sense of fascination among viewers.

Geomanist for Titles

The brand chooses to use Geomanist as the font for its titles. Geomanist is a modern, sleek, and geometric font that combines straight lines and curves in a well-balanced manner, creating a clear and contemporary impression. The simplicity and precision of this font align with Aether's pursuit of purity, transparency, and agility. Geomanist's letterforms are clear and easy to read while possessing a certain uniqueness. By using the Geomanist font, Aether's titles visually attract attention and convey the brand's modern and stylish aesthetic.

Montserrat for Texts

The brand uses Montserrat as the font for its text. Montserrat is a versatile and widely popular font known for its clean and legible appearance. It offers a harmonious balance between modernity and elegance, making it a suitable choice for Aether's brand identity. Montserrat's letterforms are well-spaced and easily readable, providing a pleasant reading experience across different mediums. By utilizing Montserrat as the text font, Aether achieves a consistent and professional look, enhancing the overall aesthetic appeal of its brand communications.

Art Direction

The reference art style of the Aether brand can be described as modern, minimalist, and exquisite. The brand emphasizes purity and simplification, striving for elegant designs that retain individuality.

Aether's art style combines geometric shapes, flowing lines, and minimalist contours to create a clean and precise visual effect. The brand primarily opts for neutral color palettes such as black, white, and gray, accentuating the purity and modernity of the designs. Additionally, Aether incorporates metallic tones like gold, silver, or rose gold to add a touch of luxury and sophistication.

In terms of artistic expression, Aether focuses on simplification and precision, aiming to convey profound thoughts and meaning in the most concise manner. The designs often incorporate natural elements, using abstract and fluid forms to express reverence and inspiration from nature.
